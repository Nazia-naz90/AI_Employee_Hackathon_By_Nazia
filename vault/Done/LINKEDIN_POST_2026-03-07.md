Senior devs: what's the career advice you wish you had at year 3? 💭

Mine (now that I'm deep into agentic AI): 

"Invest early in systems thinking over syntax knowledge."

I spent too long optimising for knowing more languages and frameworks.
The real leverage came from understanding:
→ How to decompose complex systems
→ How to design for failure
→ How to build trust incrementally

These compound over time in ways that language expertise doesn't.
